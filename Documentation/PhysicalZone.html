class PhysicalZone

A kind of TriggerZone which modify the velocity/acceleration of whatever objects are contained in them

Methods

int next ()get the next item in the physical zone's internal list. The list is updated every tick, and ordered by the UUIDs of the objects. if this happens during a script then the internal iterator will be moved to as close as possible to the previous index without going under.
reset ()reset the iterator to the beginning of the list.
